Our story

About the club

People and dogs learning together, serving together and having a remarkably good time along the trail.

Founded around a simple idea

The Susquehanna Trail Dog Training Club was founded in May 2004 by dog trainer Dixon Cuff. His vision was an organization that strengthened the bond between owner and canine and used that bond to better the community.

Today, the club continues under the leadership of Connie Cuff, with beginning and advanced members participating in weekly classes and community activities.

What members do

  • Beginning and advanced obedience classes
  • Canine Good Citizen preparation
  • Therapy visits to hospitals, nursing homes, schools and prisons
  • Community demonstrations, tricks and dog square dancing
  • Reading-to-the-dogs programs with local schools
A trained dog is only part of the story. The real goal is a stronger relationship at the other end of the leash.
